Therapy for boundaries, self-trust, and secure attachment

Wynn Therapy is a New York–based virtual private practice for high-achieving, emotionally insightful adults who are ready to understand themselves on a deeper level.

Elizabeth supports clients navigating dating fatigue, breakups, low self-worth, and boundary struggles often rooted in emotionally immature or narcissistic family dynamics and intergenerational trauma.

This work helps you feel more confident, grounded, and secure in relationships… with yourself and others.

Together, we connect the dots between your past and present so you can stop second-guessing yourself, choose partners differently, and build a more secure, grounded sense of who you are.

Meet Elizabeth Wynn

Creative Arts Therapist in New York

Elizabeth Wynn is a Licensed Creative Arts Therapist based in Brooklyn, NY, with over nine years of experience across diverse New York City mental health settings, including public schools, intensive outpatient programs, supportive housing, and community-based work with adults, children, and families.

These experiences shaped her deeply compassionate, culturally responsive approach and her belief that every person has the capacity to heal.

In her virtual practice, she offers collaborative, strength-based therapy that helps clients make sense of their experiences, access their inner resources, and create meaningful, lasting change.
