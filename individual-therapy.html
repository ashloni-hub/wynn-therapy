How I work

Individual Therapy for Insight, Healing, and Lasting Change

You might be high-functioning on the outside, but internally you feel anxious, stuck, burned out, or unsure. Individual therapy gives you space for deeper understanding and exploration of your past experiences, family dynamics, and cultural identity.

My approach is collaborative, strength-based, and tailored to you as an individual.

I integrate talk therapy and creative arts therapy. My work is grounded in the belief that you already have the capacity for healing.

Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer in-person or virtual sessions?

All sessions are provided virtually via HIPAA-compliant Zoom for clients located in New York State.

What is the session fee?

Sessions are $150 each.

Do you accept insurance?

I'm in-network with Cigna, Aetna, and Carelon Behavioral Health. I also offer superbills for out-of-network clients and partner with Thrizer.

How often will we meet?

Most clients attend weekly sessions.

Do I need to use art in creative arts therapy?

No — incorporating art into sessions is always optional.

How long does therapy last?

This varies by individual goals, needs, and pace — we'll check in regularly on how it's going.
