Therapy for boundaries, self-trust, and secure attachment

Wynn Therapy is a New York–based virtual private practice for high-achieving, emotionally insightful adults ready to understand themselves deeper. Elizabeth supports clients with dating fatigue, breakups, low self-worth, and boundary struggles often rooted in emotionally immature or narcissistic family dynamics.

Together, we connect the dots between your past and present so you can stop second-guessing yourself, choose partners differently, and build more secure grounding.
